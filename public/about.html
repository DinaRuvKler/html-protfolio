About Me

My professional journey began in the world of aesthetics; I hold a Practical Engineer degree in Architecture and Interior Design and have also specialized in User Experience (UX) Design.

This unique background allows me to approach software development with a keen eye for detail, structure, and usability. Currently, I am fully immersed in my coding studies, eager to build intuitive and efficient applications that combine functionality with great design.

When I am not at my computer, you will likely find me tending to my garden and plants, or baking fresh pastries in the kitchen.
